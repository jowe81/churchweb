Services list

The list on the left shows your church's services. Click on any of them to look at the service plan, review rehearsal information, download music etc.

If instead you click on the label of an open position that you'd like to make yourself available for, the respective leader may schedule you into the service (if they do, you will receive notice).

For your convenience, services that involve you are highlighted.

You can navigate the timeline by clicking any of the links on the navigation bar.
